# ConchClub
## Movie Club Objective

A project to manage and facilitate a movie club. Movie Club operates semi-regularly on a weekly schedule.
Movie club is a social gathering where members submit a single movie per individual every season to be watched together. On occasion seasons are themed but in general the club is open to any movie submission.

## Goals
The goals of this project should be to facilitate the following:
- Season Management by administrator
    - Basic CRUD operations
    - Season should be locked after submissions are closed
    - Seasons may have a theme that should be displayed
- Movie submissions by users
  - Submissions should not be editable after the season is locked
- Random selection and reveal of movies
    - Movies should be easy to reveal by and admin
    - Movies should be easy to remove
    - Submissions will remain a surprise until an admin chooses to reveal the selection for movie club
